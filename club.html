Description

This club is meant for 9-12th grade students with little to no programming experience. We'll have fun learning how to make a basic HTML website and the basics of programming in Python by way of some cool coding activities.

Club Rules

  • You may use your own laptop or a school laptop (in-club only).
  • You may only work on club related topics on your laptop during our club meetings.
  • There are no due dates. Work at your own pace but you should set goals.
